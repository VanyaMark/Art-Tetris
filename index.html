ART TETRIS

An evil thief has been stealing paintings from all over the world.

Luckily, after many months of arduous investigation he was finally caught by the FBI.

They found his secret hideout. His treasures included money, jewels and, of course, many precious paintings.

There were soo many paintings that it was impossible to sort them out!

Now it's your turn to help FBI order them by art periods and return them to the desperate museum managers all over the world!

Use left and right arrow to move the painting, use Enter to drop it faster.
